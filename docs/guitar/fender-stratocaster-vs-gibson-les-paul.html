Fender Stratocaster vs Gibson Les Paul

Two different voices. The Fender Stratocaster is bright, glassy and percussive with the famous in-between "quack". The Gibson Les Paul is thick, warm and powerful with rolled-off highs and pushed mids.

Fender Stratocaster

bright, glassy and percussive with the famous in-between "quack".

Pickups: three single-coils

Best for: blues, funk, surf and bright rock

Gibson Les Paul

thick, warm and powerful with rolled-off highs and pushed mids.

Pickups: two humbuckers

Best for: rock, hard rock and metal
